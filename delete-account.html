👋 Want to delete your account?

We're sorry to see you go! If you'd like to delete your MAK account and all associated data, we make it easy. Follow the steps below — your account will be permanently removed within 7 days of your request.

📧 How to Request Deletion

Send an email from your registered email address to:

📧 Request Account Deletion

Please use the subject line: "Delete my MAK account"

In the email, include:

  • Your registered email address (the one you signed up with)
  • Your display name (as shown in the app)
  • (Optional) Reason for leaving — your feedback helps us improve!

📋 What Gets Deleted

When we delete your account, we permanently remove:

Data TypeWhat Happens
Email addressPermanently deleted
Display namePermanently deleted
Password (hashed)Permanently deleted

💖 Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered. If you wish to use MAK again, you'd need to create a new account.

⏱️ How Long Does It Take?

  1. Within 24 hours: We'll send a confirmation email acknowledging your request.
  2. Within 7 days: Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our servers.
  3. Within 30 days: Any backup copies are also wiped, completing the deletion.

🤔 Just Want to Pause?

Not ready to delete entirely? You can also:

  • Uninstall the app — your account stays but you stop using it (no new data is collected)
  • Change your password — secure your account if you're worried about access
  • Email us your concerns — we'd love to know what we can fix
